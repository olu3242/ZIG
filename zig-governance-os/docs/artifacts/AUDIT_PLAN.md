# Artifact: Audit Plan

## Purpose
Defines scope, sampling approach, and timeline before an internal audit begins — the
artifact that makes an audit defensible rather than ad hoc.

## Backing Data
`AuditService` (`packages/services/src/AuditService.ts`) — real, exists on `main`.

## Structure
- Audit scope (which assets/controls/frameworks are in scope)
- Sampling methodology and rationale
- Timeline and milestones
- Auditor assignments

## Track
Audit

`docs/learning/lessons/audit/01_AUDIT_FOUNDATIONS.md` through
`03_*` (scope and sampling lessons)

## Lab
`docs/learning/labs/AUDIT_LAB_EXECUTE_INTERNAL_AUDIT.md`

## Skill
Scoping an audit and justifying a sampling methodology before fieldwork starts.

## Career Outcome
Can plan an internal audit that withstands an auditor's own challenge of its scope.
