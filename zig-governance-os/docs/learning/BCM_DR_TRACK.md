# Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Track

Trains learners on continuity planning using risk data and scenario simulation — backed by
`RiskService` and `ScenarioService` (`scenarios`, `scenario_runs`).

## Learning path: Business Continuity Planning

| Module | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Business Impact Analysis Fundamentals | lesson | 25 min |
| Recovery Time and Recovery Point Objectives | lesson | 25 min |
| From Risk Register to Continuity Plan | lesson | 30 min |
| Run a Disruption Scenario for ManufacturX | lab | 60 min |
| Score ManufacturX's Recovery Readiness | exercise | 45 min |

## Notes

- Maps to `RiskService` (criticality/severity inputs) and `ScenarioService.findRuns`
  (`ScenarioRun.scoreDelta` as the recovery-readiness measure).
- Final exercise uses the ManufacturX scenario (`docs/scenarios/MANUFACTURX.md`).
