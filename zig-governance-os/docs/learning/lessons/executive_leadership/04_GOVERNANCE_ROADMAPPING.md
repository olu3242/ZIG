# 04_GOVERNANCE_ROADMAPPING

## Objectives
- Build a 12-month governance roadmap tied to specific score-improvement milestones.
- Sequence roadmap items by dependency (e.g. evidence coverage before audit readiness).
- Set a roadmap review cadence with the board.

## Business Context
A board report (Lesson 03) is a snapshot; a roadmap is the plan that makes the next
snapshot better. This lesson builds the forward-looking half of executive communication.

## Scenario Mapping
GovSec (`docs/scenarios/GOVSEC.md`) — roadmap closing its "Respond Function Controls"
gap and formalizing board reporting.

## Framework Mapping
Trains on sequencing `Recommendation` records by dependency and projected
`GovernanceScore` impact.

## Diagram Requirements
- 12-month governance roadmap timeline
- Roadmap dependency sequencing diagram

## Knowledge Check
1. Why must evidence coverage improvements typically precede audit-readiness milestones on a roadmap?
2. What should trigger a mid-cycle roadmap revision?
