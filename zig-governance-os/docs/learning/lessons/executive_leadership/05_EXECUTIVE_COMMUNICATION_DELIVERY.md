# 05_EXECUTIVE_COMMUNICATION_DELIVERY

## Objectives
- Deliver a board report verbally in under 10 minutes without losing accuracy.
- Field hard questions about a governance score without becoming defensive or evasive.
- Know when to say "I don't know, I'll follow up" rather than guess in front of a board.

## Business Context
This closes the Executive Leadership track — the best-built report (Lessons 03-04) still
fails if delivered poorly. This lesson is presentation and live Q&A handling.

## Scenario Mapping

(paired with ZARA's Hiring-Manager/Mentor persona for live feedback, see `docs/coaching/`).
