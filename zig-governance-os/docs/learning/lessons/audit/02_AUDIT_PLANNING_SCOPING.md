# 02_AUDIT_PLANNING_SCOPING

## Objectives
- Scope an audit to a specific framework, project, and time window.
- Select which controls and evidence are in-scope vs. out-of-scope.
- Set realistic audit timelines based on evidence availability.

## Business Context
A poorly scoped audit either misses material gaps or burns weeks on irrelevant controls —
this lesson trains disciplined scoping before fieldwork begins.

## Scenario Mapping
HealthBridge (`docs/scenarios/HEALTHBRIDGE.md`) — scope an ISO 27001 internal audit ahead
of its next external HIPAA assessment.

## Framework Mapping
Trains on `Audit.frameworkId`, `Audit.startsAt`/`endsAt`, and `ProjectFramework` scoping.

## Diagram Requirements
- Audit scoping decision tree
- Scope boundary diagram (in/out-of-scope controls)

## Knowledge Check
1. What information determines whether a control is in-scope for a given audit?
2. Why does audit timeline depend on evidence availability, not just control count?
