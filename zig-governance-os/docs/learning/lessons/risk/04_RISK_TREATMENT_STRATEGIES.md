# 04_RISK_TREATMENT_STRATEGIES

## Objectives
- Choose between avoid, mitigate, transfer, and accept for a given scored risk.
- Justify a treatment decision against cost, residual risk, and business priority.
- Document who has authority to accept a given severity of risk.

## Business Context
A scored risk with no treatment decision is inert. This lesson connects scoring (Lesson 03)
to action, and ties back to Governance Track's decision-rights lesson.

## Scenario Mapping
ManufacturX (`docs/scenarios/MANUFACTURX.md`) — decide treatment for its top-scored risks.

## Framework Mapping
Trains on `RiskAssessment.treatmentDecision` and the `RiskTreatment` union.

## Diagram Requirements
- Risk Treatment Decision Flowchart
- Treatment-by-severity authority matrix

## Knowledge Check
1. When is "transfer" (e.g. insurance) a more appropriate treatment than "mitigate"?
2. Why does "accept" require a documented decision-rights holder?
