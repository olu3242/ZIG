# 02_RISK_IDENTIFICATION

## Objectives
- Run an asset-driven risk identification workshop.
- Write a risk statement with a clear asset, threat, and consequence.
- Avoid duplicate/overlapping risk entries for the same underlying exposure.

## Business Context
Identification quality determines everything downstream — a vague risk statement produces
a vague assessment and an untestable control. This lesson is about writing risks well.

## Scenario Mapping
ManufacturX (`docs/scenarios/MANUFACTURX.md`) — identify risks across its two SCADA
network assets.

## Framework Mapping
Trains on `RiskService.create` (via `BaseService`) and `Risk.title`/`description`.

## Diagram Requirements
- Risk identification workshop flow
- Risk statement quality checklist (good vs. bad example diagram)

## Knowledge Check
1. What three components must a well-formed risk statement contain?
2. Why is "ransomware" alone not a valid risk statement?
