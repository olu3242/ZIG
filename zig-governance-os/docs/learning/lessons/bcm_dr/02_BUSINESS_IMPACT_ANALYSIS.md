# 02_BUSINESS_IMPACT_ANALYSIS

## Objectives
- Run a business impact analysis (BIA) on a critical asset, quantifying downtime cost over time.
- Identify dependencies between assets that compound impact.
- Translate BIA output into a prioritized continuity plan order.

## Business Context
A BIA is what turns "this system is critical" into a number leadership can act on —
this lesson builds that quantification skill directly.

## Scenario Mapping
ManufacturX (`docs/scenarios/MANUFACTURX.md`) — BIA on its Plant 1 SCADA Network asset.

## Framework Mapping
Trains on `Asset.criticality` as BIA input and dependency mapping across `Asset` records.

## Diagram Requirements
- BIA downtime-cost curve diagram
- Asset dependency map

## Knowledge Check
1. What does a BIA quantify that a simple criticality rating does not?
2. Why do asset dependencies compound impact rather than add linearly?
