# Audit Lab: Execute Internal Audit

## Scenario
HealthBridge (`docs/scenarios/HEALTHBRIDGE.md`).

## Inputs
- HealthBridge's EHR Integration Service asset, its access-logging risk, and requested evidence
- Audit track Lessons 02-04 (Planning & Scoping, Evidence Sampling, Findings & Corrective Actions)

## Tasks
1. Scope an internal ISO 27001 audit against HealthBridge's HIPAA-relevant controls.
2. Define an evidence sampling plan for its access-logging evidence request.
3. Execute fieldwork (simulated) and write at least one defensible finding.
4. Draft a corrective action tied to a `Task`, with owner and target date.

## Deliverables
- Audit Plan + at least one finding with corrective action.

## Scoring Rubric
| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Audit scope correctly excludes out-of-scope controls | 20% |
| Sampling plan rationale is defensible (size justified by criticality/population) | 25% |
| Finding contains observation, criteria, cause, and effect | 30% |
| Corrective action has an owner, target date, and links to a real gap | 25% |

## AI Feedback Rules
ZARA reviews as **Auditor**: rejects any finding missing one of the four required
components, and as **Reviewer**: checks the corrective action's target date is realistic
given the finding's severity.
