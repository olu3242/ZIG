# Risk Lab: Create Enterprise Risk Register

## Scenario
ManufacturX (`docs/scenarios/MANUFACTURX.md`).

## Inputs
- ManufacturX's two SCADA network assets and their criticality
- Risk track Lessons 01-03 (Foundations, Identification, Assessment & Scoring)
- `RiskSeverity`/`RiskTreatment` type unions

## Tasks
1. Identify at least 5 risks across ManufacturX's two plant assets, each with a well-formed risk statement.
2. Score each risk's likelihood and impact (1-5) with written rationale.
3. Derive severity for each risk from its likelihood/impact score.
4. Build a risk heatmap of the resulting register.

## Deliverables
- Risk Register with 5+ scored risks, rationale, and a heatmap.

## Scoring Rubric
| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Every risk statement has asset, threat, and consequence | 30% |
| Likelihood/impact scores have written, defensible rationale | 30% |
| Derived severity matches the likelihood × impact mapping consistently | 20% |
| Heatmap accurately reflects the register's severity distribution | 20% |

## AI Feedback Rules
ZARA reviews as **Instructor**: corrects any risk statement missing asset/threat/
consequence, and as **Auditor**: challenges any severity score whose rationale doesn't
justify the stated likelihood or impact value.
