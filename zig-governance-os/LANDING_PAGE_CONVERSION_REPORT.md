# Landing Page V4 Conversion Report

Date: 2026-06-19

## Conversion Objective

Move first-time visitors from curiosity to package selection by making the category, audience, capabilities, outcomes, and buying path clear.

## Primary Audience

- Governance teams
- Compliance teams
- Risk and audit teams
- Consultants and advisors

## Conversion Improvements

1. Clear category creation:
   - The hero defines Zig as a Governance Operating System.
2. Faster comprehension:
   - The first capability section summarizes the platform in five scannable cards.
3. Less overpromising:
   - Future-state autonomous positioning is removed from the main landing narrative.
4. Stronger buying path:
   - Pricing is reduced to Starter, Professional, and Enterprise.
5. Better product mental model:
   - The workspace visual shows Mission Control, Frameworks, Risk Workspace, Evidence Workspace, Learning Center, and Executive Reporting.
6. Better audience mapping:
   - Four audience cards explain primary use cases and expected outcomes.

## CTA Strategy

- Start Free appears in hero, header, pricing, and final CTA.
- Book Demo appears in hero and final CTA.
- Professional is marked Most Popular for team buyers.

## Risk Controls

- No prominent Digital Twin positioning.
- No autonomous compliance claims.
- No self-healing runtime claims.
- No predictive governance claims.
- AI is positioned as assisted workflow support.

## Conversion Readiness

Status: READY FOR MVP TRAFFIC after deployment and environment remediation are complete.
