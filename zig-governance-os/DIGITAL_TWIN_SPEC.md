# Executive Digital Twin Specification

The executive digital twin provides a real-time representation of organizational compliance health and forecasts posture over 90, 180, and 365 day horizons.

## Implementation

- Package: `packages/digital-twin`
- Web route: `apps/web/app/digital-twin`
- Engine: `ExecutiveDigitalTwin`
- Twin components: governance, risk, compliance, audit, vendor, policy, certification
- Outputs: target gap and horizon forecast
